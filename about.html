Our Mission:
At AquaTraceWeb, our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge of water consumption linked to everyday products. By understanding the water footprint of products, we aim to foster informed choices that contribute to a sustainable future.

Why Water Footprint?
Every drop of water we consume leaves an invisible trace behind. Whether it's the water used to grow our food, produce our clothes, or manufacture our devices, understanding the volume and source of water consumed provides a window into the impact of our choices. As global water scarcity becomes an increasing concern, awareness of our individual and collective water footprints is more crucial than ever.

Our Journey:
Founded in 2023 by a dedicated team of six mechatronics students from Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology, AquaTraceWeb began as an ambitious idea in a college project.

Witnessing the challenges of global water scarcity and recognizing the importance of understanding water footprints, we felt compelled to make a difference. This drive and passion to combine our technical knowledge with our commitment to environmental sustainability propelled AquaTraceWeb from a concept to a platform that now serves countless users.

Our goal has always been clear: to illuminate the hidden water footprints behind the products we use daily and foster a more conscious and sustainable consumer behavior.

What We Do:
We provide users with accurate, research-backed data on the water footprints of thousands of products. From the food we eat to the clothes we wear, our database offers insights into the water consumed throughout the lifecycle of products.

Join Us:
Be a conscious consumer. Every choice we make has an impact. By being aware of the water footprints of the products we consume, we can make choices that are not only good for us but also for our planet. Join us on this journey to a more sustainable future!
